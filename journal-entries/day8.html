Journal Entry - Day 8

Date: October 13, 2024

Reflections:

The day today has passed by incredibly quickly. I have been drifting without much purpose. I have done very little of anything productive, and I have not even rested. I have, though, followed all training precepts. I have feelings of sadness and slight anxiety—old,  familiar feelings. I cannot say much else, and there is nothing to improve. May we all be happy.
