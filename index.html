Welcome to My Journey

This blog will document my journey from October 6, 2024, to July 6, 2025. Each day, I will reflect on the path as I follow these nine precepts:

  1. Abstain from killing, causing the killing, or rejoicing in the killing of any living being.
  2. Abstain from taking anything that has not been given.
  3. Abstain from causing harm through speech, including gossip, lying, hiding the truth, or using harsh language.
  4. Abstain from all sexual conduct.
  5. Abstain from consuming intoxicants.
  6. Abstain from any form of entertainment and bodily decoration.
  7. Follow the one-session practice of eating only in one session.
  8. Sleep only during the first and second part of the night.
  9. Meditate for two hours daily.
